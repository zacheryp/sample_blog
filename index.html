What is The Odin Project?

The Odin Project bills itself as "a free open source coding curriculum that can be taken entirely online".

What does the Odin Project curriculum cover?

The Odin Project is comprised of a Foundations program that is common to all learners. Following that, learners can choose to focus on developing in Ruby or Javascript.
